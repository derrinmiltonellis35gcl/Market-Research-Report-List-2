<p><p>Data converters play a vital role in converting analog signals to digital signals and vice versa. ADC (Analog-to-Digital Converter) market focuses on converting continuous analog signals into discrete digital signals, enabling accurate data processing in applications like sensors, communication devices, and medical instruments. On the other hand, DAC (Digital-to-Analog Converter) market deals with converting digital signals back into analog form, ensuring high-quality audio reproduction in devices like smartphones, music players, and audio equipment. Both markets are essential for ensuring seamless communication and data processing in various industries.</p></p>
