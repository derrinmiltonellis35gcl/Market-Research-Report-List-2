<p><p>Organic Banana Powder is available in two main market types: Freeze Dried and Spray Dried. Freeze Dried banana powder is made by freeze drying fresh bananas, which helps retain their nutrients and flavor. On the other hand, Spray Dried banana powder is made by spraying a banana puree into a fine mist and drying it quickly. Both types of banana powder are popular in the market for their convenience, long shelf life, and versatility in various food and beverage applications.</p></p>
