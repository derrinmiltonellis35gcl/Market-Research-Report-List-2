<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Photonic IR Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Wave Infrared</li><li>Mid Wave Infrared</li><li>Long Wave Infrared</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photonic IR sensors are categorized based on their wavelength sensitivity: Short Wave Infrared (SWIR), Mid Wave Infrared (MWIR), and Long Wave Infrared (LWIR). SWIR sensors detect wavelengths ranging from 0.9 to 1.7 microns, suitable for imaging through fog and smoke. MWIR sensors operate in wavelengths of 3 to 5 microns, ideal for detecting heat signatures. LWIR sensors cover wavelengths of 8 to 14 microns, commonly used in thermal imaging applications. Each type has its specific applications and advantages in various industries.</p></p>
