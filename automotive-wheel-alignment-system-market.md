<p><p>Automotive Wheel Alignment System market types include Toe-In / Toe-Out Alignment System, Camber Alignment System, Caster Alignment System, and Four Wheel Alignment System. Toe-In / Toe-Out Alignment System refers to the measurement of the difference in distance between the front and back of the tires. Camber Alignment System measures the angle of the tire in relation to the road surface. Caster Alignment System measures the angle in which the wheel is positioned in relation to the vehicle. Four Wheel Alignment System encompasses all three measurements for comprehensive alignment adjustments.</p></p>
