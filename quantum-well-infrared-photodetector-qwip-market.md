The Light Guide Type QWIPs use a light guide to direct the infrared radiation onto the detector, improving sensitivity and performance. On the other hand, Photovoltaic Type QWIPs convert incoming infrared radiation directly into an electrical signal, making them more efficient in terms of power consumption. Both types of QWIPs cater to different applications and requirements in the market, offering varied benefits to users.</p></p>
