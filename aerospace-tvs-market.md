<p><strong>The Aerospace TVS Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uni-polar TVS</li><li>Bi-polar TVS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aerospace TVS (Transient Voltage Suppressor) market includes two main types: Uni-polar TVS and Bi-polar TVS. Uni-polar TVS devices are designed to protect against voltage spikes in one direction only, making them ideal for applications where protection is needed in one polarity. Bi-polar TVS devices, on the other hand, provide protection against voltage spikes in both directions, offering a more comprehensive solution for applications where voltage fluctuations can occur in multiple polarities. Both types play a crucial role in safeguarding aerospace electronics from potential damage due to transient voltage surges.</p></p>
