<p><strong>The Military Fiber Optic Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Mode</li><li>Multimode-Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Military fiber optic cable market can be divided into two main types: single-mode and multimode. Single-mode fiber optic cables are designed to transmit data over long distances with minimal signal loss, making them ideal for military applications requiring high bandwidth and secure communication. On the other hand, multimode fiber optic cables are better suited for shorter distance communication within military installations or on battlefield, as they can transmit multiple signals simultaneously. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring efficient and reliable communication within military operations.</p></p>
