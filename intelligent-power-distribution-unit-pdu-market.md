The market for PDUs is divided into two main types: single-phase and three-phase. Single-phase PDUs are typically used in smaller installations and provide power through a single voltage phase, while three-phase PDUs are used in larger installations and deliver power through three separate voltage phases. Both types of PDUs offer advanced features such as remote monitoring and control for efficient power management.</p></p>
