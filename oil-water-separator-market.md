<p><p>Oil water separators are used to remove oil and other contaminants from water in various industries. There are three main types of oil water separators based on the method used for separation: mechanical based, chemical based, and membrane based. </p><p>- Mechanical based separators use physical processes such as gravity, flotation, coalescence, and filtration to separate oil from water. </p><p>- Chemical based separators use chemicals to accelerate the separation process by inducing coagulation or breaking up emulsions. </p><p>- Membrane based separators use membranes to selectively filter out oil and contaminants from water.</p></p>
