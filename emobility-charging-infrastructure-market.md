DC charging infrastructure, on the other hand, uses direct current to charge electric vehicles at a much faster rate, making them more convenient for long-distance travel. Both types of charging infrastructure are essential for the growth of the emobility sector and play a crucial role in supporting the transition to electric vehicles.</p></p>
