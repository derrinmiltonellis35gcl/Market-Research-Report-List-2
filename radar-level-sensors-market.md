<p><ul><li>Through-Air</li><li>Guided-Wave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radar level sensors are commonly used in industry to measure the level of liquids or bulk solids in tanks or vessels. There are two main types of radar level sensors - Through-Air and Guided-Wave. Through-Air sensors transmit radar signals through the air to measure the level of the substance. Guided-Wave sensors, on the other hand, use a probe that directs radar waves along a rod or cable to measure the level. Both types of sensors are reliable and accurate options for measuring levels in various industrial applications.</p></p>
