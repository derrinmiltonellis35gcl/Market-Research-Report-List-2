<p><p>Furniture drawer systems come in two main types: single walled and double walled. Single walled drawers are made of one layer of material, typically plywood or particleboard, and are more budget-friendly. Double walled drawers feature an additional layer of material, making them sturdier and more durable. They are usually made of a combination of materials like wood, steel, or aluminum. While single walled drawers may be suitable for lighter use, double walled drawers are preferred for heavy-duty applications and areas where extra durability is needed.</p></p>
