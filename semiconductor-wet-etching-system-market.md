<p><p>Semiconductor wet etching systems are used for etching different materials in semiconductor processing. Dielectric etching involves the selective removal of dielectric materials such as silicon oxide or nitride. Silicon etching is the process of etching silicon wafers to create patterns or structures. Metal etching is the removal of metal layers on semiconductor devices. Other types of wet etching systems may include compound semiconductor etching or glass etching. Each type of etching system is designed to meet specific requirements for different semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
