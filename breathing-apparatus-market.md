<p><ul><li>Open-Circuit</li><li>Closed-Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Breathing apparatus market is divided into two main types: open-circuit and closed-circuit systems. Open-circuit apparatus release exhaled air into the environment, while closed-circuit systems recycle exhaled air, removing carbon dioxide and replenishing oxygen. Open-circuit apparatus are commonly used in industrial and firefighting applications due to their simplicity and lower cost, while closed-circuit systems are preferred in military and deep-sea diving due to their ability to conserve oxygen and remove contaminants from the air. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and well-being of users in hazardous environments.</p></p>
