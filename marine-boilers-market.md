<p><p>Marine boilers can be categorized into two main types: Fire Tube Boilers and Water Tube Boilers. Fire Tube Boilers have tubes filled with water and are heated externally by hot gases, transferring heat to the surrounding water. Water Tube Boilers have water-filled tubes that are heated internally by combustion gases, increasing efficiency and allowing for higher pressure levels. Both types of boilers play a crucial role in providing heat and power for various marine applications, including propulsion and electricity generation.</p></p>
