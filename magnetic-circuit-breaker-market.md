<p><p>Magnetic circuit breakers are categorized into single-pole and multi-pole types based on their configuration. Single-pole magnetic circuit breakers are designed to interrupt a single circuit, while multi-pole magnetic circuit breakers are capable of simultaneously interrupting multiple circuits. Single-pole circuit breakers are commonly used in residential applications where individual circuits need to be protected, while multi-pole circuit breakers are often used in industrial or commercial settings where multiple circuits need to be controlled and monitored.</p></p>
