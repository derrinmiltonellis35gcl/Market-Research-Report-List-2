<p><ul><li>Two-Way</li><li>Three-Way</li><li>Four-Way</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pneumatic valves are categorized into two-way, three-way, and four-way types based on their functionality. Two-way valves have one inlet and one outlet for controlling the flow of air in a single direction. Three-way valves have one inlet and two outlets, allowing for diversion of airflow. Four-way valves have two inlets and two outlets, enabling control of air flow in two different directions. Each type of valve serves a specific purpose in pneumatic systems, providing flexibility and control over airflow.</p></p>
