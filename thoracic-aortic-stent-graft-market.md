<p><p>The Thoracic Aortic Stent Graft market includes two main types: metal and polymer. Metal stent grafts are typically made of materials like stainless steel or nitinol, offering durability and strength for stabilization of the aorta. On the other hand, polymer stent grafts are made of flexible materials such as polyester or ePTFE, providing a more malleable option that conforms better to the shape of the aorta. Both types have their own advantages and are used based on the patient's specific needs.</p></p>
