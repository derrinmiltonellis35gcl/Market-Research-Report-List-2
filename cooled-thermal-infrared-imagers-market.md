<p><p>Cooled thermal infrared imagers are used in various industries for thermal imaging applications. There are two main types of technologies used in these imagers: Vanadium Oxide (VOx) and Amorphous Silicon (A-Si). VOx technology offers high sensitivity and resolution, making it ideal for military and defense applications. On the other hand, A-Si technology is more cost-effective and suitable for commercial applications such as building inspections and surveillance. Both technologies play a crucial role in the thermal imaging market.</p></p>
