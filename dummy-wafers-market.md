<p><p>Dummy wafers are used in semiconductor manufacturing for process monitoring and equipment calibration. There are two main types in the market: Single Side Polished Dummy Wafers and Double Side Polished Dummy Wafers. Single Side Polished Dummy Wafers have one side polished for flatness, while Double Side Polished Dummy Wafers have both sides polished. These dummy wafers mimic the properties of actual wafers but do not contain any electronic components. They are essential for ensuring the accuracy and efficiency of semiconductor production processes.</p></p>
