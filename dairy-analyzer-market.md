<p><p>Dairy analyzers are used in the dairy industry to quickly and accurately analyze the composition of milk and dairy products. Two common types of dairy analyzers are ultrasonic and infrared analyzers. Ultrasonic analyzers use sound waves to measure the density of the milk, while infrared analyzers use infrared light to analyze the fat, protein, and lactose content of the sample. Both types of analyzers are used by dairy farmers and producers to ensure the quality and consistency of their products.</p></p>
