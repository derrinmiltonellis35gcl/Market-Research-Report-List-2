The 802.15.4 market focuses on low-power, low-data-rate applications, commonly used in IoT devices. WiFi SoCs are designed for high-speed wireless internet connectivity in various devices like smartphones, laptops, and smart home appliances. Bluetooth SoCs are widely used for short-range communication between devices, such as wireless headphones, fitness trackers, and smartwatches. Each market type caters to different connectivity needs and applications.</p></p>
