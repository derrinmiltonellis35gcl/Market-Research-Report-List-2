<p><p>RF tappers are commonly categorized based on their insertion loss, measured in decibels (dB). Tappers with up to 5 dB insertion loss are ideal for applications where minimal signal attenuation is critical. Tappers with 5 to 10 dB insertion loss are suitable for general RF signal splitting. Tappers with 10 to 15 dB insertion loss offer moderate attenuation for signal splitting. Tappers with 15 to 20 dB insertion loss provide significant signal attenuation for specialized applications requiring precise signal distribution. Each market type caters to specific RF signal splitting requirements.</p></p>
