<p><p>The electric scrubber dryer market can be divided into two main types: walk-behind scrubber dryers and stand-on/ride-on scrubber dryers. Walk-behind scrubber dryers are manually operated machines that are pushed from behind, offering more control and precision in narrow or obstructed spaces. Stand-on/ride-on scrubber dryers, on the other hand, allow the operator to stand or sit on the machine while driving it, making them ideal for larger areas or for users who require a faster and more efficient cleaning process.</p></p>
