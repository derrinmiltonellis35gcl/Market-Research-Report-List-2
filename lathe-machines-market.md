<p><p>Lathe machines are categorized into two main types: vertical lathes and horizontal lathes. Vertical lathes have a vertical spindle axis and are ideal for handling large, heavy workpieces that require turning on their sides. On the other hand, horizontal lathes have a horizontal spindle axis and are commonly used for smaller workpieces that require turning on their ends. Both types of lathe machines have their own set of advantages and are used in various industries for machining operations.</p></p>
