<p><ul><li>Double layer Helical CT Scanner</li><li>Multi Layer Helical CT Scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Helical CT scanners are classified into two main types, namely double layer helical CT scanners and multi-layer helical CT scanners. Double layer helical CT scanners utilize two sets of detectors to acquire images simultaneously, which results in improved image quality and faster scanning times. On the other hand, multi-layer helical CT scanners have multiple rows of detectors, allowing for even faster scanning speeds and higher resolution images. Both types of helical CT scanners are widely used in medical imaging for diagnosing various medical conditions.</p></p>
