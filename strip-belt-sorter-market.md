<p><p>Strip belt sorters are commonly classified into three different market types based on their degrees of sorting angles: 30°, 45°, and 90°. In a 30° market, products are sorted at a gentle angle, allowing for smooth and efficient sorting of items with minimal disruption. The 45° market offers a slightly steeper sorting angle, ideal for larger or heavier items that require a bit more force to be redirected. The 90° market provides the most aggressive sorting angle, perfect for quickly diverting items to their designated destinations.</p></p>
