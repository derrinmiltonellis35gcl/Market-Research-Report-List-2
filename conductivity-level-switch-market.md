<p><strong>The Conductivity Level Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Electrode</li><li>Suspended Electrode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>A Solid Electrode conductivity level switch is a type of sensor that uses a metallic rod as the sensing element to detect changes in conductivity. It is commonly used in applications where the liquid being measured is clean and free of debris. On the other hand, a Suspended Electrode conductivity level switch uses a probe that is immersed in the liquid to measure conductivity. This type of sensor is ideal for applications where the liquid may contain solid particles or debris.</p></p>
