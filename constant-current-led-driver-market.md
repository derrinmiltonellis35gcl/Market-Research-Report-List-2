<p><strong>The Constant Current LED Driver Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Boost Type</li><li>Buck Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Constant current LED drivers are available in two main types: Boost Type and Buck Type. </p><p>Boost Type LED drivers increase the voltage to deliver a constant current to the LED, making them ideal for applications where the input voltage is lower than the LED's forward voltage. </p><p>On the other hand, Buck Type LED drivers decrease the voltage to maintain a constant current, making them suitable for applications where the input voltage is higher than the LED's forward voltage.</p><p>Both types have their own advantages and are used in various lighting applications.</p></p>
