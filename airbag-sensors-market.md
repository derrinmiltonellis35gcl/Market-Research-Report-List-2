<p><p>Airbag sensors are vital components in vehicle safety systems, helping to detect impact and deploy airbags quickly. Front sensors are located in the front of the vehicle and monitor head-on collisions. Rear sensors are positioned at the back of the vehicle and detect rear-end impacts. Knee sensors are situated in the lower dashboard area and activate airbags to protect the driver's knees. Side sensors are placed on the sides of the vehicle and respond to side collisions, deploying airbags to protect occupants from injury.</p></p>
