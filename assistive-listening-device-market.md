<p><p>The assistive listening device market consists of various types of products to aid individuals with hearing impairments. Infrared hearing systems use infrared signals to transmit audio to wireless receivers worn by users. Hearing loop systems utilize magnetic induction to broadcast sound directly to hearing aids equipped with telecoils. Amplified telephone systems enhance the volume of phone calls for those with hearing difficulties. Personal hearing amplifiers are wearable devices that amplify sounds for individuals with mild to moderate hearing loss. Other devices include FM systems and captioned telephones.</p></p>
