<p><p>Centralized Lubrication Systems Market Types include Single-line Type, Double-line Type, and Multi-line Type. </p><p>Single-line Type systems distribute lubricant through a central pump to each lubrication point one at a time. Double-line Type systems utilize two main lines to deliver lubricant in alternating cycles. Multi-line Type systems have multiple lines connected to a central pump, allowing for simultaneous lubrication of multiple points. Each type offers unique advantages in managing lubrication needs in various industrial applications.</p></p>
