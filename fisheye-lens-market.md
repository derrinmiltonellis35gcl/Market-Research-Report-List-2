<p><p>Fisheye lenses are categorized into two main types based on their image projection: circular fisheye lens and diagonal fisheye lens. Circular fisheye lenses produce a circular image with a 180° field of view, resulting in a distorted, rounded effect. On the other hand, diagonal fisheye lenses produce a rectangular image with a 180° field of view and less distortion at the edges. Both types are popular among photographers and videographers for their unique and creative visual effects.</p></p>
