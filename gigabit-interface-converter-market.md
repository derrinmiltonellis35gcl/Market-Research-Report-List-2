<p><p>Gigabit Interface Converter (GBIC) market is categorized into two main types: Multi-Mode GBIC and Single-Mode GBIC. Multi-Mode GBICs are designed for shorter distances and utilize LED technology for transmission. They are commonly used in local area networks (LANs). Single-Mode GBICs, on the other hand, are designed for longer distances and utilize laser technology for transmission. They are typically used in wide area networks (WANs) and data center connections. Both types play a crucial role in expanding network capabilities and improving data transmission speeds.</p></p>
