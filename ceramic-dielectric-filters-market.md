<p><strong>The Ceramic Dielectric Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bandstop Filters</li><li>Band-pass Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic dielectric filters are used in various markets, including bandstop and band-pass filters. Bandstop filters are designed to attenuate signals within a specific frequency range, allowing only signals outside this range to pass through. On the other hand, band-pass filters are used to only allow signals within a specific frequency range to pass through, while attenuating signals outside this range. Both types of filters are essential in applications where precise control over signal frequencies is required, such as in wireless communication systems.</p></p>
