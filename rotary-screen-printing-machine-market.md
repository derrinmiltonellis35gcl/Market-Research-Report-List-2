<p><p>Rotary screen printing machines are available in three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines require operators to manually feed the fabric and control the printing process. Semi-automatic machines automate some processes, such as the feeding of fabric, but still require some manual intervention. Fully automatic machines are the most advanced, with automated fabric feeding, printing, and drying processes. Each type of machine caters to different production needs and levels of automation, providing options for various printing businesses.</p></p>
