<p><p>Single Screw Press and Twin Screw Press are two main types in the Screw Press market. Single Screw Press consists of one screw that rotates within a cylindrical cage, while Twin Screw Press has two intermeshing screws that rotate together to apply pressure. Single Screw Press is more common and cost-effective, suitable for a wide range of applications. On the other hand, Twin Screw Press offers higher efficiency and throughput, making it ideal for heavy-duty or specialized operations.</p></p>
