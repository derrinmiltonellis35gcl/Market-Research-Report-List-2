<p><ul><li>Distributed Sensing</li><li>Fiber Optic Point Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber optic sensing solutions are divided into two main market types: distributed sensing and fiber optic point sensing. Distributed sensing involves the continuous measurement of physical parameters along the length of a fiber optic cable, providing real-time monitoring of large areas. On the other hand, fiber optic point sensing allows for the measurement of specific points along the cable, offering higher resolution and accuracy for localized monitoring. Both market types cater to a wide range of industries including oil and gas, civil engineering, and healthcare.</p></p>
