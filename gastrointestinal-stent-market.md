<p><p>Gastrointestinal Stent Market Types include Fully Covered, Partially Covered, and Non-Covered options. Fully Covered Gastrointestinal Stents are fully encapsulated in a silicone or polymer coating, providing maximum protection and reducing the risk of tissue ingrowth. Partially Covered Gastrointestinal Stents have an uncovered portion to allow tissue ingrowth for better anchoring, while still providing some protection with a covering. Non-Covered Gastrointestinal Stents do not have any coating, allowing for maximum tissue ingrowth but increasing the risk of complications such as migration.</p></p>
