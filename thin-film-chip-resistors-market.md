<p><p>Thin film chip resistors are electronic components used in circuits to provide resistance to the flow of electric current. The growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for electronic devices and advancements in technology leading to the development of smaller and more efficient resistors. The market is also witnessing a rise in demand for high precision resistors with low TCR (temperature coefficient of resistance) and high stability.</p><p>One of the key trends in the thin film chip resistors market is the growing adoption of surface mount technology (SMT) in electronics manufacturing, which requires smaller and more reliable components like thin film chip resistors.
