<p><p>Electrically Tunable Focusing Lenses (ETFL) are revolutionary optical devices that can change their focal length by applying an electric field. There are two main market types for ETFL: Flat Convex and Flat Concave. Flat Convex lenses have a flat surface on one side and a convex curvature on the other, allowing them to converge light rays. In contrast, Flat Concave lenses have a flat surface on one side and a concave curvature on the other, enabling them to diverge light rays.</p></p>
