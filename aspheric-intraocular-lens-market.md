<p><p>The aspheric intraocular lens market is divided into two types: rigid and foldable. Rigid intraocular lenses are made of a solid material and provide stable optical performance. They are typically used in procedures where precise vision correction is required. Foldable intraocular lenses are made of flexible material that can be inserted through smaller incisions, resulting in faster recovery times and reduced risk of complications. Both types of lenses aim to improve vision after cataract surgery and correct refractive errors.</p></p>
