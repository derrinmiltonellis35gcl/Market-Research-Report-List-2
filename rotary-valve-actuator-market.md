<p><strong>The Rotary Valve Actuator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pneumatic</li><li>Hydraulic</li><li>Electric</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rotary valve actuators are classified into four main types based on their operation: pneumatic, hydraulic, electric, and others. Pneumatic actuators utilize compressed air to generate torque, making them suitable for fast and reliable valve control. Hydraulic actuators use fluid pressure to actuate the valve, providing strong and precise control. Electric actuators rely on electric power to operate the valve, offering efficient and consistent performance. The remaining "others" category includes unique actuators such as electro-hydraulic or manual handwheel actuators. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on specific application requirements.</p></p>
