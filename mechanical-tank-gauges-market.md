<p><p>Mechanical tank gauges are widely used in various industries to measure the level of liquids in tanks. The different types of mechanical tank gauges include tubular type, reflection type, transparent type, bicolor type, and others. Tubular type gauges use a simple glass tube to provide a visual indication of the liquid level. Reflection type gauges use a float to reflect light to indicate the level. Transparent type gauges allow direct viewing of the liquid level. Bicolor gauges use two different colors to indicate the level, while other types offer unique features for specific applications.</p></p>
