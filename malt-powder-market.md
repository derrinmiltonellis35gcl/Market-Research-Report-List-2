<p><strong>The Malt Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Diastatic</li><li>Diastatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Malt powder is available in two main types: Non-Diastatic and Diastatic. Non-Diastatic malt powder is processed to remove the enzymes responsible for breaking down starches in grain, making it suitable for adding flavor and color without affecting dough rise. On the other hand, Diastatic malt powder contains active enzymes that help with fermentation and dough development, resulting in improved texture and crust in baked goods. Both types offer unique characteristics and applications in the food industry.</p></p>
