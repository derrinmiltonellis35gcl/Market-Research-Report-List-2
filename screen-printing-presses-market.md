<p><p>Screen printing presses are categorized into three main types based on the shape and design of the press: flat-bed screen printing presses, cylinder screen printing presses, and rotary screen printing presses. Flat-bed presses are stationary with the screen moving up and down, cylinder presses have a rotating cylindrical screen, and rotary presses have a continuously rotating screen. Each type has its own advantages and is used for different applications, such as printing on flat surfaces, cylindrical objects, or continuous rolls of material.</p></p>
