<p><p>Material Transfer Vehicles (MTVs) are divided into two main types: Crawler Type and Wheel Type. Crawler Type MTVs are designed with tracks that provide better traction and maneuverability on rough or uneven terrains. They are commonly used in large construction projects. On the other hand, Wheel Type MTVs feature wheels for easy mobility on smooth surfaces and are more suitable for smaller construction sites or road maintenance projects. Both types of MTVs play a crucial role in efficiently transferring and distributing materials on construction sites.</p></p>
