<p><p>Steering position sensors are essential components in vehicles that provide feedback on the position of the steering wheel. There are two main types of steering position sensors available in the market - magnetic sensors and contacting sensors. Magnetic sensors use magnetic fields to detect the position of the steering wheel, while contacting sensors use physical contact with the wheel to determine its position. Both types have their advantages and are commonly used in modern vehicle systems for accurate steering control and safety.</p></p>
