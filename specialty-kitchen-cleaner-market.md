<p><p>Specialty kitchen cleaners can be categorized into two main market types: Neutral Cleaner and Alkaline Cleaner. Neutral cleaners are non-acidic and are designed to remove general soil, grease, and grime without harming surfaces. They are ideal for regular cleaning and maintenance. On the other hand, Alkaline cleaners are more heavy-duty and are formulated to tackle tough grease and baked-on food residues. They are commonly used in commercial kitchens or for deep cleaning tasks. Both types of cleaners play a crucial role in maintaining a clean and hygienic kitchen environment.</p></p>
