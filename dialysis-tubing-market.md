<p><p>Dialysis tubing market types include regenerated cellulose, cellulose acetate, polyacrylonitrile, ethylene-vinyl alcohol copolymer, and others. Regenerated cellulose tubing is cost-effective and widely used, while cellulose acetate tubing offers good biocompatibility. Polyacrylonitrile tubing is known for its high permeability, and ethylene-vinyl alcohol copolymer tubing has excellent mechanical properties. Other materials used in dialysis tubing include polysulfone and polyethylene terephthalate. Each type of tubing has specific benefits and is chosen based on the requirements of the dialysis procedure.</p></p>
