<p><p>Semiconductor MEMS probe cards are used for testing semiconductor devices during production. Vertical MEMS probe cards consist of vertical probes that make contact with the device under test. Cantilever MEMS probe cards use cantilever probes that bend to make contact. Vertical MEMS probe cards offer higher density and better performance, while cantilever MEMS probe cards are more flexible and can adapt to different test requirements. Both types cater to the demand for precision testing in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
