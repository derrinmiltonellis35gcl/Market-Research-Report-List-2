<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Samplers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Automatic</li><li>Probe</li><li>Vacuum</li><li>Composite</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Samplers market can be categorized into various types based on their mode of operation. Automatic samplers are programmed to collect samples at specific intervals without human intervention. Probe samplers use a sampling probe to collect samples from a specific location. Vacuum samplers utilize suction to draw samples into a collection vessel. Composite samplers combine multiple samples into a single representative sample. Other types of samplers may include grab samplers, which collect instantaneous samples, and passive samplers, which absorb contaminants over time. Each type of sampler has its own advantages and applications in different industries.</p></p>
