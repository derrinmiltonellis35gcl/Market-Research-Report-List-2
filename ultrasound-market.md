<p><p>The ultrasound market can be divided into two main types: diagnostic ultrasound and therapeutic ultrasound. Diagnostic ultrasound is primarily used for imaging internal organs and tissues to diagnose medical conditions. It is widely used in various medical specialties such as obstetrics, cardiology, and musculoskeletal imaging. On the other hand, therapeutic ultrasound involves the use of ultrasound waves to treat various medical conditions like muscle and joint pain, tissue repair, and bone fractures. Both types of ultrasound play crucial roles in modern healthcare practices.</p></p>
