<p><p>All-In-One Cloth Diapers come in two main types: Single-layer and Multi-layer. Single-layer diapers consist of a single absorbent layer, making them thinner and less bulky. They are ideal for light to moderate wetting. On the other hand, Multi-layer diapers consist of multiple absorbent layers stitched together, providing higher absorbency and better leak protection. These diapers are suitable for heavy wetters and overnight use. Both types offer eco-friendly and cost-effective alternatives to disposable diapers, making them popular choices among environmentally conscious parents.</p></p>
