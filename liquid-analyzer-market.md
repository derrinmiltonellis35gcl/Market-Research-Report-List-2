<p><p>Liquid analyzers are devices used to measure various properties of liquids for process control and water quality monitoring. Turbidity analyzers measure the cloudiness of a liquid, while pH/ORP analyzers measure acidity or alkalinity. TOC and COD analyzers measure organic and inorganic contaminants, respectively. Titration analyzers measure chemical concentrations through titration. Conductivity analyzers measure the ability of a liquid to conduct electricity. Chlorine analyzers measure chlorine levels in water. Dissolved oxygen analyzers measure the amount of oxygen in a liquid. Each type of liquid analyzer serves a specific purpose in monitoring and controlling liquid properties.</p></p>
