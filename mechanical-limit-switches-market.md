<p><p>Mechanical limit switches are commonly classified into two types: multiple and single. Multiple mechanical limit switches consist of multiple contacts that can be actuated at varying positions, providing more flexibility in detecting different points of travel. On the other hand, single mechanical limit switches have a single contact that is actuated at a specific point, making them suitable for simple on/off applications. Both types cater to different needs depending on the complexity of the system and the requirements of the application.</p></p>
