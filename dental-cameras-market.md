<p><ul><li>Dental Intraoral Cameras</li><li>Extraoral Cameras</li><li>Dental Digital Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental cameras are divided into three main types: dental intraoral cameras, extraoral cameras, and dental digital cameras. Intraoral cameras are designed to capture detailed images of inside the mouth, aiding in diagnostics and patient education. Extraoral cameras are used to capture images of the face and teeth from outside the mouth, useful for documenting treatment progress. Dental digital cameras combine both intraoral and extraoral capabilities, offering high-quality images that can be easily stored, edited, and shared digitally.</p></p>
